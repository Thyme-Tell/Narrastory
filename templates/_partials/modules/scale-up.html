Always Here to Help

Narra is more than just an interviewer—it's your storytelling companion. If you hit a creative block, we’re here to help you get back on track and keep capturing your memories. With Narra's powerful Search + Filter tools, you can easily locate your stories by who was involved, when they happened, and who contributed to them.

Slow down and
smell the roses.

Narra is the opposite of social media, designed to be used in an unhurried, intentional, and thoughtful way.

Whereas social posts are seen mostly by strangers, your memories are only for those with whom you genuinely want to share.

While posts are added to a feed intended for infinite scrolling, your Narra stories are paginated so your readers can enjoy your wonderful memories as they would a favorite book.
